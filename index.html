Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our custom carpentry, furniture, and cabinetry services.

Do you provide installation services for the custom furniture and cabinetry you build?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all custom furniture and cabinetry we build. Our team ensures that each piece is securely and accurately installed in your space, guaranteeing a perfect fit and finish. Installation is included as part of our comprehensive service to deliver a hassle-free experience.

What types of materials do you use for your custom carpentry projects?

We use a variety of high-quality materials for our custom carpentry projects, including solid wood (such as oak, pine, and mahogany), engineered wood, plywood, MDF, and laminates. The choice of material depends on your preferences, budget, and the specific requirements of your project. We always ensure that the materials selected are durable and suitable for the intended use.

What is your process for designing and building custom furniture or cabinetry?

Our process begins with a free consultation to understand your needs and preferences. We then design custom solutions and provide detailed drawings or sketches for your approval. Once the design is finalized, we source quality materials and begin crafting your furniture or cabinetry in our workshop. After completion, we deliver and install the finished product, ensuring everything meets your expectations.

Do you offer free consultations or quotes for custom carpentry projects?

Yes, we offer free consultations and quotes for all custom carpentry projects. Contact us to discuss your requirements, and we'll provide a detailed estimate based on your needs.

What types of custom carpentry services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of custom carpentry services including kitchen units, wardrobes, custom furniture, stands, shelves, tables, and chairs. Our team can also assist with furniture restoration, interior woodwork, and bespoke woodworking projects tailored to your specific requirements.

How long does it take to complete a custom carpentry project?

The timeline for a custom carpentry project depends on its size and complexity. Most small projects can be completed within 1–2 weeks, while larger or more detailed work may take several weeks. We provide an estimated completion date after discussing your requirements and confirming the design.
